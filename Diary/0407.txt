Spring Study Day 1
Content: Launching Hello World in Spring
Problem: localhost:8080 could accessed, but localhost:8080/ex00 was 404
Try1. Trying Tomcat instead of Pivotal tc server => Fail. (Server Tomcat v9.0 Server at localhost failed to start)

Try3. delete the contents of ‘/Users/hyeongyunmun/.m2/repository’ => Success
